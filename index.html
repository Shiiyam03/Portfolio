Project 2

CHROMA EYE

This is the project done for colour blind persons. Colour blind person cannot differentiate the colours between green and red, so that they suffer lot in traffic signals also in differentiating green apple and red apple. By using this application green objects will be shown in white colour, so that the colour blind person can identify that the object is in green colour.
